My story

I did not follow a straight road into technology.

I grew up in Nepal, came to the United States, earned my computer science degree, and built a new life through persistence.

My career moved from full-stack development into the Microsoft Power Platform, where I found work that fits how I think. I like taking a process that feels confusing or slow and turning it into something people can actually use.

In 2022, I joined the U.S. Army Reserve. Today, I serve as a Sergeant. Military service has made me a calmer leader, a better teammate, and someone who takes responsibility seriously.

At home, I am a proud husband. My wife is working toward her Doctor of Pharmacy degree. We are building our careers together, supporting each other through the long days and celebrating every step forward.

“Build a useful life. Do work that matters. Stay close to the people who matter most.”
